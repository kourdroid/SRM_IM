## 2024-05-18 - Reclamation Badge Border
**Learning:** Status badges in the SRM design system use tinted backgrounds without borders, EXCEPT for the 'Reclamation' (warning) status, which uniquely requires a 1px border to visually distinguish its warning state from standard open/closed states.
**Action:** When implementing or updating status badges, always apply a 1px solid border matching the text color exclusively for 'warning' (Reclamation) variants.
