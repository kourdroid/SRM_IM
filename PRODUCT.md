# PRODUCT.md

## Product Name
SRM (ONEE Incident Management System)

## Product Purpose
A field incident management system for ONEE (Office National de l'Electricité et de l'Eau Potable, Morocco). Field agents report and track electrical infrastructure incidents (BT and MT networks) in real-time. The system operates offline-first; agents work in rural areas with unreliable connectivity.

## Register
product

## Users

### Primary: Field Agents (Techniciens de terrain)
- ONEE electrical technicians in Moroccan provinces
- Work outdoors in varying light conditions (bright sun, dusk, vehicle dashboards)
- Limited technical literacy; must be fast and mistake-proof
- Frequently operate with one hand (holding equipment, flashlight)
- Age range: 25–55
- Languages: French interface, Darija spoken input

### Secondary: Administrators (Responsables de zone)
- Zone managers overseeing multiple field agents
- Office-based, desktop or tablet use
- Need rapid triage, status overview, and resolution metrics
- Fluent in French; administrative vocabulary

## Brand Personality
- **Industrial utility**: this is a work tool, not a consumer app. Reliability over delight.
- **Institutional authority**: ONEE is a national utility. The interface should feel governmental and serious, not startup-playful.
- **High visibility**: the accent color (#DAF22C, electric lime) must work as a safety-vest marker in bright outdoor conditions. It is functional, not decorative.
- **Quiet confidence**: dense information, no wasted space, no animations that delay task completion.

## Anti-references
- Consumer social apps (Instagram, TikTok): too playful, too much whitespace
- SaaS dashboard clichés: gradient hero cards, bouncing counters, glassmorphism
- Material Design defaults: too rounded, too colorful, too generic for an institutional tool
- Dark mode neon aesthetics: not appropriate for a governmental utility
- Overly minimalist design-agency portfolios: too precious for field conditions
